Concept

Kill Cubes Kill Cubes is a tactical shooter set within a controlled simulation, where geometric anomalies begin to disrupt system stability. Through Hardware-Integrated Combat, players interact with the game using a ESP32 controller, enabling a more direct and precise one-to-one connection between physical action and in-game response.

Key Mechanics

- The game is locked to your ESP32 controller and 3 Axis Gyroscope Sensor. If you tilt the controller in real life, the camera tilts in the game. To hit a target, you must physically point at it.

- The more Cubes that stay on screen, the more the "Virtual Dream Space" is occupied.

- You are the "Anchor" of the system. You can't run away, you have to stand your ground and use your 360-degree rotation to stop the Cubes from surrounding you.

Background Story

In a future where people spend their lives in a perfect digital "Dream Space," the system has started to decay. This decay manifests as Red Cubes, corrupted blocks of data that consume everything they touch. You play as a System Cleaner. Armed with a weapon (your controller), you are "plugged in" to the core of the dream. Your job is simple, keep the system clean, and don't let the Cubes touch the core. If you fail, the dream ends.
