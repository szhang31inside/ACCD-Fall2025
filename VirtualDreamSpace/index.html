Virtual Dream Space

Overview

This scene is set in a deep, infinite void, inspired by the isolation of a distant lunar landscape. An uneven terrain stretches across the space, interrupted by a few constructed elements. I also included a control tower, a geometric dome, and scattered mechanical objects. The scene explores the contrast between natural forms and artificial structures.

Concept Sketch

The initial sketch reflects my early idea of the space I wanted to create. I also looked at reference images from NASA to better understand what elements to include and how the environment could feel.

Building Process: Terrain & Assets

Using Unity's Terrain tools, I drew and sculptured the terrain to create a uneven surface (tried to make it different than the envrionment on earth).

Then, I downloaded a space asset from the Unity Asset Store and explored placing different objects into the scene. I also added a space sky map to emphasize the environment. During the process, I mainly explored the sizes of different objects.

Setting up Cameras & Interactive System

To ground the player in the experience, I implemented first-person controls in the scene. This allows for immersive exploration from a 1:1 human perspective. I also added a second camera to provide a general view of the space, allowing me to switch between first-person and overview modes as needed.

After adjusting the cameras, I created an UI interface before the player can enter my space.

Now, the player needs to press "START" to unlock my scene. I think this created a more mysterious atmosphere.

First-Person Perspective

From the astronaut's point of view, the scale of the environment becomes apparent.
